When

Are you asking when you can contribute your own original content? There's no constrains or any ridiculous process required to get your account up for content submission. You can start making your own content right away. See the How.

How

Start by signing up, you can do this by clicking on the sign up button at the top navigation bar.It's free and you can contribute to the site by writing articles. After signing up, you need to login to your account. You can do this by clicking on the sign in button at the top navigation bar. To confirm a successful login, you should see your name at the top right corner of the page. Your account is not yet an author untill you explicitly provide your details that align with the content of article you intend to write. You can submit your details by navigating to the authors page and clicking on Become an Author button.
